Contributors

Following people participated in jXLS evolution and making jXLS more mature and useful.

  • Leonid Vysochyn - The founder of the project. Core jXLS engine, jx tags implementation. jxls-reader module.
  • Nistor Adrian - Patch for .xlsx support
  • Michael Zarillo - Full JEXL support implementation. Many jx tags improvements and code optimizations.
  • Curtis Stanford - Added aggregate functions support to ${...} syntax and introduced 'select' keyword to forEach tag
  • Rafael Borges - Started Maven 2 migration
  • Balachandar Nageswaran - Reported many jXLS issues

Many thanks to all of them! Also thanks to all those people who had enough patience to evaluate this product! We will continue to work on making jXLS more useful and suitable for most user needs.
